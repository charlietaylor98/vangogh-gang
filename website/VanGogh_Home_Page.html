VanGogh as a "Tortured Genius"

This website details the work of a research project seeking to analyze the artwork and mental state of Dutch impressionist painter Vincient VanGogh. It was created by Charlie Taylor, Colin Woelfel, Nate McDowell and Rachel Suela-all students in the Spring 2020 section of Dr. David Birnbaum's and Dr. James Pickett's "Computational Methods in the Humanities" class.
